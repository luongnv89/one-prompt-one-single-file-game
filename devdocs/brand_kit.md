# Brand Kit: AI One-File Arcade

## Brand Overview

**Brand Mission:**
To create a clean, safe, and joyful space where anyone can explore AI-generated single-file games. The brand enables transparency, creativity, and learning by showcasing AI’s ability to build delightful microgames from a single prompt.

**Brand Personality:**

- Minimalist
- Playful
- Trustworthy
- Friendly
- Educational

**Target Audience:**
AI hobbyists, indie developers, educators, students, game jam organizers, and tech enthusiasts exploring AI-generated games in a safe, transparent, and lightweight environment.

**Brand Positioning:**
AI One-File Arcade stands out as a **minimalistic, security-first, AI-only game gallery**. Unlike generic game platforms, it emphasizes **AI transparency**, **single-file simplicity**, and **safe sandboxing**, making it ideal for both experimentation and education.

# Color Palette

The brand is anchored around **green** as the primary color — chosen for its associations with creativity, innovation, growth, and playfulness, while remaining gentle and non-intrusive in a minimalist environment.

## Primary Colors

### **Primary Color — Emerald Green**

- **Hex:** `#34D399`
- **RGB:** `rgb(52, 211, 153)`
- **HSL:** `hsl(156, 72%, 52%)`
- **Usage:**
  Primary CTAs, highlights, key interactions (Play button), active states, links.
- **Rationale:**
  Green conveys creativity, freshness, and innovation. It’s widely associated with “go/play” concepts—perfect for a game arcade. It also stands out against neutral backgrounds without feeling aggressive.

### Primary Variants

- **Primary Light:** `#6EE7B7`
  (Hover states, soft backgrounds)
- **Primary Dark:** `#059669`
  (Pressed states, shadowed depth)
- **Primary Lighter:** `#ECFDF5`
  (Subtle surfaces, disabled backgrounds)

## Accessibility Guidelines

- Text on primary (`#34D399`): Use **Black (#0C0C0C)** or **Gray 900 (#111827)** for ≥ 4.5:1 contrast.
- Text on white backgrounds: Use **Gray 900 (#111827)**.
- Text on dark backgrounds: Use **White (#FFFFFF)**.
- All contrast combinations tested meet **WCAG 2.1 AA** standards.

# Logo Guidelines

## Logo Style: Playful Minimalism

The AI One-File Arcade logo uses a **simple geometric icon + wordmark**, with subtle playful energy:

- Icon resembles a **tiny retro game screen** made of a square + a green pixel.
- Wordmark: **Roboto Bold**, spaced wide, clean, friendly.

## Logo Variants

- **Primary Logo:** Green icon + black wordmark
- **Secondary Logo:** White-on-black, for dark sections
- **Small Icon:** Square pixel icon only (favicon, app icon)

## Usage Rules

- **Clear Space:** At least **1× icon width** around all edges.
- **Minimum Size:** 32px height (digital), 0.5in (print).
- **Color Options:**
  - Green + Black
  - White on Black
  - All-black

## Forbidden Modifications

- No stretching or skewing
- No off-brand colors
- No shadows, bevels, gradients
- Do not place on noisy background
- Do not rotate

# Appendix

## AI Research Insights

### Round 1 — Color Psychology & Competitor Analysis

- Green is associated with innovation, creativity, and “go/play.”
- Competitors like itch.io use red/purple; js13kGames uses orange/black.
- Green differentiates the arcade strongly.

### Round 2 — Typography

- Roboto is highly legible and widely supported.
- Great for documentation + UI.
- Superior web performance vs heavier fonts.

### Round 3 — Analysis of Similar Brands

- Minimalistic dev tools (Vercel, Figma, Notion) rely on neutral palettes + a single accent color.
- This aligns with the arcade’s minimalist identity.

### Round 4 — Accessibility Validation

- All chosen colors pass WCAG AA.
- Typography scale ensures readability.

### Round 5 — Holistic Review

- Brand is consistent across product, UX, and PRD.
- Scalable for solo dev.
- Visual identity supports transparency + playful simplicity.
